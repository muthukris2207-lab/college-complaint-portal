Sri Krishna Arts and Science College, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu

Overview

Founded in 1997, Sri Krishna Arts and Science College (SKASC) is an autonomous university in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. The university is known for its academic excellence and is accredited with "A" grade by NAAC and ranked 37th among Indian universities as per NIRF Rankings 2024. With state-of-the-art infrastructure and a student-centric approach, SKASC provides a vibrant learning environment that fosters academic and personal growth. The university offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs, including popular courses such as B.Sc. Computer Science, BCom Professional Accounting, BBA, BCA, MCom Admission is primarily based on merit, with some courses requiring a certain minimum score. 


The fee structure is affordable, ranging from Rs 16,000- 43,000 for all programs. SKASC also supports students with scholarships based on merit and financial need. SKASC has a good placement track record, with top recruiters like Accenture, Cognizant, IBM, HP, and Amazon hiring its graduates. The highest package offered by SKASC is 16 LPA, while the average package is around 5 LPA. Admissions for the academic year 2025 are yet to be decided.

Hostel

Sri Krishna Arts and Science College (SKASC) offers excellent hostel facilities to provide students with a safe, comfortable, homely environment. The campus features separate hostels for men and women, equipped with modern amenities to ensure a convenient stay for the residents. The men’s hostel spreads across an area of 71,852 sq. ft. and can accommodate up to 680 students. Whereas the women’s hostel, covering 26,860 sq. ft., accommodates 550 students.

The hostels offer well-ventilated rooms, hygienic dining services, 24/7 security, Wi-Fi, laundry facilities, recreation rooms, and study halls. Separate hostels for men and women ensure comfort and safety.
